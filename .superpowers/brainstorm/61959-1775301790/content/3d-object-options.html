What should the 3D entity look like?

Replacing the Spline robot. Still tracks cursor, but now represents AI/intelligence.

A

Neural Orb

A glowing sphere with visible internal particle connections -- like synapses firing. Pulses and shifts color when the chat AI is "thinking." Minimal, elegant, futuristic. Think: a contained universe of thought.

Vibe: Clean, premium, sci-fi

B

Wireframe Brain / Neural Network

A stylized 3D brain or network graph with nodes and edges. Nodes light up in sequence when processing. More literal representation of AI/ML work. Connections animate as data "flows" through.

Vibe: Technical, literal, academic

C

Morphing Geometric Form

An abstract shape (icosahedron, toroid, or impossible geometry) that subtly morphs and deforms in response to cursor and chat state. No literal "brain" reference -- pure abstraction. Surface shaders shift between states.

Vibe: Abstract, artistic, high-end studio portfolio

D

Particle Cloud / Token Stream

A cloud of floating particles that self-organizes into different shapes. When idle, it's an ambient swarm. When the chat processes, particles rearrange into text fragments or symbols before dissolving. Directly references how LLMs process tokens.

Vibe: Dynamic, unique, conceptually deep

My recommendation

Option A (Neural Orb) -- it's the most versatile. Works as ambient decoration when idle, becomes visually active during chat, and doesn't force a literal "brain" metaphor that could feel cheesy. It's also the most feasible to build with Three.js/React Three Fiber (which is already in your deps) or as a new Spline scene. Options B and D risk looking like a CS textbook illustration if not executed perfectly.
